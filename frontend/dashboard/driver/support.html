Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my assigned trip?
Go to "Assignments" page, find your trip, and click "Start Trip" button. Make sure to check the vehicle condition before starting.
What if I have an accident?
Call our emergency hotline immediately at +250 788 123 456. Take photos of the scene and contact the customer support team.
How do I get paid?
Your earnings are calculated per trip. Payments are processed weekly and deposited to your registered bank account or mobile money.
What if the customer is late?
Wait for 15 minutes, then contact the customer via phone. If unreachable, contact support for further instructions.
How do I report vehicle issues?
Submit a support ticket with "Vehicle Issue" category. Include photos if possible. Our team will assist you promptly.
Can I reject a trip assignment?
You can reject a trip but frequent rejections may affect your rating and future assignments. Contact support for valid reasons.
