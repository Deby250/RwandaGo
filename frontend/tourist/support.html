Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my booking?
You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund.
What documents do I need?
Valid driver's license, passport, and international driving permit if required.
Is insurance included?
Yes, basic insurance is included. Premium insurance is available as an add-on.
What is the fuel policy?
Vehicles are provided with a full tank and must be returned with a full tank.
