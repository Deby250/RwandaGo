Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How do I book a car?
Simply create an account, select your pickup and return dates, choose a vehicle, and make the 20% deposit payment.
What documents do I need?
Valid driver's license, passport, and international driving permit (if required).
Is insurance included?
Yes, basic insurance is included. Premium insurance is available as an add-on.
What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. After that, deposit is non-refundable.
Can I extend my rental?
Yes, contact support at least 24 hours before your return time.
